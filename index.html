Barbarian

This fearless warrior relies on his bulging muscles and striking mustache to wreak havoc in enemy villages. Release a horde of Barbarians and enjoy the mayhem! The Barbarian is a male Scottish warrior with an angry, battle-ready expression, hungry for destruction.
Hog Rider

The Hog Rider is a bare-chested man holding a hammer. He has a Mohawk, wears a brown leather loincloth, a red belt, and a pair of leather sandals, as well as two large golden wristbands and a gold earring. His ride is a large hog, about half his height. It has a brown leather bridle looped around its tusks, which in turn is held by the Hog Rider.
Goblin

These pesky little creatures only have eyes for one thing: LOOT! They are fast and their hunger for resources is limitless.
Pekka

She wears heavy dark blue armor, has team-colored horns, eyes, and wields a large metal sword.
Wall Breaker

Wall Breaker's appearance is that of a small skeleton that wears a brown aviator hat and has a small scar across its right eye. It carries a large bomb.
